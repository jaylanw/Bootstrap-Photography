Hey there,

I’m Jaylan! Born and raised in San Antonio. I’m a part-time barista and a full-time friend. Topo Chico drinker, book reader, podcast listener, and total music lover. Technically I’m 21 years old, but if you ask any of my friends they’ll tell you I’m an oldy at heart.

I love keeping things simple. Black and white are my two favorite colors. If I were to list a few of my favorite memories they will fall under passenger seat rides and shared laughs over a home cooked meal. I learned, in order to keep life fun, it’s not always about what you do, but who you’re with. The best moments in life are the raw connections, the simple glance between lovers, the laugh between best friends, and the love between a mom and her child. I LIVE to capture the brief seconds in life where the world stops - your mind takes a mental photograph and suddenly you’re thrown back into reality.
